Feature Main

Get excited! Our Original Superfood Juice is now available in a 1L Tetra Pak carton, a lighter and more compact container that offers all the quality, freshness, and goodness you expect from Sambazon! The new TetraPak…

Keeps Juice Fresh

Sambazon juice is kept cold from the second it’s filled to the time it hits stores. Just stick it in the fridge when you get home and you’re golden.

Protects the planet

70% of the materials in our new carton are sourced through paperboard from selectively harvested, re-grown trees.

Less Packaging

Tetra Pak cartons mean less packaging and more juice! In fact, each bottle is 96% juice and only 4% packaging. More Sambazon to enjoy, less to recycle!

100% recyclable

Tetra Paks are recyclable! Check out the Carton Council website at recyclecartons.com to learn more and see if recycling is available in your community!
